Database Expert · Tool Builder · Author

I'm a DBA with deep experience in PostgreSQL and SQL Server. Over the years I've tuned thousands of slow queries, designed indexing strategies for high-traffic databases, and helped teams get more out of their database infrastructure.

I built QueryOptimizer because I was tired of not having a good local tool for diagnosing performance issues without sending data to the cloud. It's self-hosted, installs in 5 minutes, and does everything I need — EXPLAIN visualization, missing index detection, slow query tracking, and SQL anti-pattern analysis.

I'm also writing Database Mastery — a practical book that covers everything I wish I'd known earlier in my career. It covers PostgreSQL and SQL Server, real-world indexing, query rewriting, execution plans, and how AI tools are changing the DBA role.

On this blog I write about database performance, AI + databases, and the tools and techniques I use every day. If you're a DBA or developer who works with databases, you'll find something useful here.
